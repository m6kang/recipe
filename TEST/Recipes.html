Tomato Tofu


Ingredients:

  • 1 Tofu
  • 3-5 roma tomatoes
  • tbsp ketchup or tomato paste
  • 1/2 tbsp fish sauce
  • salt
  • sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 stalk of green onions

Directions:

  • dry the tofu with paper towels
  • fry tofu in neutral oil and drain on paper towel
  • cut tomatoes into 6ths
  • add everything into pan/pot, cover with a lid, bring to a boil on high heat, then reduce to 30% heat untill the tomatoes are cooked about 10 minutes total
  • taste and reseason

          Tomato Pasta


          Ingredients:

          • 3 Roma Tomatoes
          • 8 Oz Uncooked Pasta
          • 2-3 Cloves of Garlic
          • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste
          • 3/4 Cup Heavy Cream
          • Salt & Pepper
          • Parmesan

          Directions:

          • Boil a large pot of salted water
          • Cook Pasta according to instructions
          • Cut the tomatoes into small chunks
          • Melt butter in a pan and start cooking tomatoes on low heat
          • Saute garlic and tomatoes and then add heavy cream
          • Drain pasta water and put in pasta into pan
          • Add in heavy cream salt and peper
          • Add in parmesan
